Below is a brief overview of our Diamond Star Digital Visiting Card: how it works, what it offers, and what is in use today versus what is available for future use.

————————————————————————————————————————
1. HOW IT WORKS (FLOW)
————————————————————————————————————————
• Internal (our side): Staff open the main website, select the section (e.g. GREEN CITY or DSA Group) and the employee, then view that person’s card. A QR code is generated that points to that employee’s digital card. That QR can be downloaded as an image (with logo) for printing on visiting cards, posters, or email signatures.
• External (contacts/clients): When someone scans the QR with their phone, they land on that employee’s digital visiting card page. They see the employee’s photo (where we have one), name, title, company, Arabic name/title, and full contact details (mobile, office, email, address). They can call, email, or tap “Add to Contacts” to save the contact — including the photo — to their phone (iPhone and Android).

So the flow is: we generate and share the QR → they scan → they see the card and can save the contact with photo.

Profile photos for all employees where we have a photo in the system (e.g. many DSA and several Green City). Company website link and company profile PDF from the card.
